Naruto

Story

Naruto a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Masashi Kishimoto. It tells the story of Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja who seeks recognition from his peers and dreams of becoming the Hokage, the leader of his village. Friendship, Hope, and Sacrifice. Naruto (the show) establishes these three as the absolute crux of humanity. Naruto's (the character's) main focus is his friends whom he never loses hope in and would sacrifice everything for, to the point of giving himself a heart attack at the thought of losing his closest friend.

Strongest Clans

Otsutsuki Clan

The Otsutsuki Clan is the strongest of all clans in the Naruto series. Home to parasitic aliens who come from another planet, the Otsutsuki clan sees all its members go from planet to planet to consume all life and get stronger in the process. By consuming new life forms, they continuously update themselves.

Uchiha Clan

The Uchiha are descendants of Indra Ōtsutsuki, the elder son of Hagoromo Ōtsutsuki. Through Indra, the Uchiha inherited Hagoromo's "eyes", granting them powerful spiritual energy and chakra. As part of the so-called "Curse of Hatred" first displayed by Indra, t is due to the Sharingan that the Uchiha have received much of their fame and notoriety.

Uzumaki Clan

The Uzumaki Clan (うずまき一族, Uzumaki Ichizoku) was a formerly prominent clan in Uzushiogakure. Its members are renown for having bright, red hair, possessing incredibly strong life-forces and chakra, and their prowess in fūinjutsu.

Senju Clan

The Senju clan were known for their powerful abilities in ninjutsu, particularly in the use of Wood Release techniques They were also said to possess a unique genetic trait known as the "Body Flicker Technique," which allowed them to move at incredible speeds. Additionally, members of the Senju clan were known for their strong physical abilities and high levels of endurance

Hyuuga Clan

In the series, the clan is known for its possession of the Byakugan, a kekkei genkai (a unique genetic ability) that gives them the ability to see through solid objects and in a 360-degree radius around them. The clan also possesses the Gentle Fist style of combat, which uses the Byakugan to target and damage the body's chakra points. The Hyūga Clan is one of the four noble clans of the Hidden Leaf Village (also known as Konoha or the Leaf Village) and is considered to be one of the strongest clans in the series.
